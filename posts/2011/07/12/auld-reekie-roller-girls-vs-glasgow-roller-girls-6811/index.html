Auld Reekie Roller Girls Vs Glasgow Roller Girls-6/8/11

Posted: by scottishrollerderby 2011-07-12 12:01 [Source]
Tags:  arrg cannon belles grg maiden grrders upcoming bouts

Auld Reekie Roller Girls' Cannon Belles welcome Glasgow Roller Girls' Maiden Grrders to Meadowbank Sports Centre on Saturday the 6th of August for some East V West action.It's a busy month for ARRG,as they also play Stuttgart Valley Roller Girlz a few weeks later.Both bouts are part of the Edinburgh Fringe Festival.Entry £5
